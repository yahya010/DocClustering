Team Semantics User Stories:


As a person interested in politics, I would like to easily visualize frequent words/phrases appearing in congressional speeches and compare that content to previous speeches.


As a person brainstorming an idea, I would like to be shown other words/phrases related to the idea to help with my brainstorming and know if I am on the right track.


As a researcher, I would like to know the best way to break down long documents to attain useful classifications of data clusters.
